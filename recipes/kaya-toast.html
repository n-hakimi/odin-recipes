Kaya Toast

About dish

Roti Bakar is a simple yet popular breakfast and tea-time snack enjoyed throughout Malaysia. The bread is toasted until golden and crispy on the outside while remaining soft on the inside. It is often served with butter, kaya (coconut egg jam), or other spreads, making it a quick, delicious, and satisfying treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons kaya

Steps

  1. Place 2 slices of bread on a clean plate
  2. Toast the bread using a toaster.
  3. While the bread is still warm, spread butter evenly over one side on each slice.
  4. Add kaya spread on top.
  5. Cut the toast in half and serve immediately while warm.
